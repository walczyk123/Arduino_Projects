#WEMOS XI 8F328P-U

* MCU:  LGT8F328P compatibile with MEGA328P.  
	* 32kb flash
	* 2kb SRAM
	* 16 MHz clock
* Pins:
	* 16 Digital pins
		* 8 PWM pins
	* 6 ADC inputs (10 bit)
* Power:
	* via USB cable
	* 3.3V stable power source
	* 5V input
* 3.3V or 5V working voltage, can be changed with the jumper next to the regulator.
* Build-in LED and reset button
